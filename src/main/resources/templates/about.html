Our mission is to help you easily create and share your events

'Digs' was built on an idea that puts the average Joe's perspective of a quick and easy party-organizing in mind. Digs helps event promoters, individuals and event owners to create, setup, and share events with either friends, acquaintances, or the public with just a few clicks. Dig users after creating an event, can choose and control invitees, include entrance secret-code for guest, or even charge an entrance fee, depending on the type of event the event creator has in mind. Digs is a free software product created mostly with the use of open-source technology, owned and redistributed by Digital Design Revolution LLC.
